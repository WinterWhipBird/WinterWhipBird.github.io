NFT Beanie for Decentraland with Royalty Avoidance Protection

I am working towards creating a beanie that can be worn by avatars in Decentraland. Each avatar will have their own beanie. The beanie will have a image on it, which will be different for each beanier. People can buy and sell the beanies, with a royalty paid on each sale to me, the original creator of the beanie. The elements of this are shown in the diagram below. ![Plan](plan1.png)

Decentraland is a metaverse that people can use via a web browser. Wearables are items of clothing that avatars can wear. Avatars come with default wearables. People can buy or acquire other wearables for their avatar. Wearables that people own are linked to Non Fungible Tokens (NFTs), and are called Linked Wearables.

Wearables in Decentraland are 3D models. TODO need to read more about this: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4Qd3BI6IYN4

NFTs are ERC 721 tokens. These tokens are each unique. The ownership of an ERC 721 token is based on which Ethereum address is the owner of the token in the ERC 721 contract.

Decentaland allows the ERC 721 tokens to be hosted on a range of blockchains; for example Ethereum, Polygon, Binance. Transactions on Ethereum tend to cost more, as it is the most popular blockchain in terms of funds. Because of this, this is where the largest markets for NFTs are: Open Sea.
